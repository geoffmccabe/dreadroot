================================================================================
1. WHAT IS DREADROOT?
================================================================================
Dreadroot is a 3D, first-person, browser-based voxel world. You explore a
shared multiplayer world built from blocks, grow gigantic trees from seeds,
climb through them, fight tiered monsters with tiered weapons, build with
blocks, harvest fruit, collect loot, and trade. Your weapons get stronger as
you level up. You log in with your Lightningworks account.

Core loop: explore → fight monsters & level up → collect seeds, loot & coins →
plant and grow huge trees → climb/build in them → trade and progress.

================================================================================
2. GETTING STARTED
================================================================================
- Sign in on the Dreadroot login screen with "Sign in with Lightningworks."
- Click the game screen once to capture the mouse (look around). Press ESC to
  release the mouse.
- Move with W A S D (or arrow keys), look with the mouse, jump with SPACE.
- Open your Inventory with I to see your seeds, blocks, and items.
- The world is shared and persistent — what you build and grow stays.

================================================================================
3. MOVEMENT & TRAVERSAL
================================================================================
- Walk/run: W A S D or Arrow keys.
- Look: move the mouse (after clicking to capture it).
- Jump: SPACE.
- Jet boost: tap/hold SPACE again in the air to use jet boots — a limited
  boost that refills over time. Great for reaching high branches.
- Glide: press G while falling (in the air) to glide and slow your descent.
  It turns off automatically when you land.
- Sprint: hold SHIFT to move faster. Hold CTRL to crawl slowly.
- Up / Down: Q to go up, Z to go down (used when flying/in water).
- Climbing trees: there is no "climb" key — trees are solid structures you
  platform through. Jump (and jet-boost) onto branches; the game auto-steps
  you up small ledges. Big trees have climbable branches and interiors.
- Swimming works in water; use Q/Z to move up/down.

================================================================================
4. COMBAT — WEAPONS, TIERS, MONSTERS
================================================================================
- Ready your gun: press R to toggle your weapon on/off (you’ll hear it cock).
- Fire: with the gun ready, LEFT-CLICK to shoot. Aim with the crosshair.
- Weapon tiers: your bullet power scales automatically with your player level
  (roughly level 1–30 maps to weapon tier 1–10). You do NOT manually set your
  tier — level up by playing/fighting and your weapon gets stronger and
  changes color by tier. Special items/power-ups can boost damage or tier.
- Weapon types include a standard gun, a multi-shot "pentabullet," and a
  tiered flamethrower. Higher tiers = more damage.
- Monsters: the world has several monster families, each with many tiers
  (tougher, higher-tier variants): Shnakes, Shombies, Shtickmen, Shwarms,
  and Walapas. Shoot them to defeat them; higher-tier monsters take more or
  stronger hits. Defeating monsters helps you progress and can drop loot.
- If a question asks exact monster HP/damage or which weapon "beats" which
  monster: that isn’t specified here — advise experimenting or asking a mod.

================================================================================
5. TREES — SEEDS, PLANTING, GROWING, CHOPPING
================================================================================
Getting seeds:
- From your Inventory (press I), the in-game Market (press O), the player
  Marketplace (press M), and by chopping down your own trees to get the
  seed back. Loot/drops can also yield items.

Planting a tree:
- Press T to enter tree-planting mode. Use [ and ] to cycle through your
  available seeds, then CLICK where you want to plant.
- Special tree types: within ~3 seconds of pressing T,
    • press 2 (T then 2) for a WIDE tree
    • press 3 (T then 3) for a FUNGAL tree
  In wide/fungal planting mode, [ and ] cycle those seed types.
- Press T again to exit planting mode.

Growing:
- Trees grow over real time after you plant them — you don’t water or tend
  them. Growth continues on the server and is resumable; come back later and
  the tree will have grown. Different seeds/tiers grow into different sizes
  and shapes (some become massive, 300+ blocks tall, with climbable interiors).

Chopping / recovering a seed:
- You can chop down a tree you own: face one of its blocks and repeatedly
  hit it (Minecraft-style hold-to-chop — about 5 chops). A confirmation
  appears, and destroying your tree returns its seed to your inventory so
  you can replant elsewhere.

Fruit:
- Grown trees can produce fruit. Press F to harvest the nearest fruit for a
  reward. (Exact rewards aren’t listed here.)

================================================================================
6. BUILDING WITH BLOCKS
================================================================================
- Press B to toggle building mode.
- In building mode: [ and ] cycle your block types; CLICK to place the
  selected block (and to remove blocks). Blocks come from your inventory and
  are consumed when placed.
- Use blocks to build structures, bridges, defenses, and to reach places.
- Press B again to exit building mode.

================================================================================
7. ITEMS, ECONOMY & PROGRESSION
================================================================================
- Inventory (I): your seeds, blocks, and items.
- Market (O): buy blocks/items in-game.
- Marketplace (M): the player marketplace for trading/listings.
- You earn Coins and Points through play; the game also supports token
  balances. (Exact prices/economy details aren’t specified here.)
- Collectibles: glowing/special blocks and wisp blocks can be collected;
  shrines are special highlighted tree blocks.
- Your profile/avatar: open the user panel (your avatar/user menu in the
  on-screen UI) to set your display name, upload an avatar image, adjust
  view distance/fog, and to Log Out.

================================================================================

  LEFT-CLICK .............. Plant seed / place or remove block (in that mode)

================================================================================
9. QUICK TIPS
================================================================================
- Level up by fighting monsters — your weapon automatically gets stronger.
- Plant seeds early; trees take real time to grow, so come back later.
- Out of seeds? Chop one of your own trees to get its seed back, or buy
  more in the Market (O) / Marketplace (M).
- Use jet boost (SPACE in air) + glide (G) to climb and explore tall trees.
- Stuck/mouse won’t move? Click the game screen to recapture the mouse;
  ESC releases it.
